Starting Point

A digital asset model requiring legal structure across real estate, virtual assets, and investor protection

This matter involved advising on the structuring of a real-world asset tokenization platform in the UAE, focused on the fractionalization of real estate interests in Dubai within an evolving virtual assets regulatory environment.

The challenge was to build a framework capable of supporting tokenized ownership while remaining aligned with developing regulatory standards. The model needed to address real estate ownership mechanics, potential securities considerations, virtual asset regulation, investor protection, and the broader question of how a technology-led platform could operate compliantly within overlapping legal regimes.

Strategic Considerations

Balancing innovation, licensing, ownership structure, and regulatory risk

Investor Protection
